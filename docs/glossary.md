# Glossary

This document defines key terms used in Förmögenhetsanalys.

## A

**Asset**: A resource of economic value, including real estate, listed equity, unlisted equity.

**Atkinson Index**: A measure of inequality that incorporates social welfare considerations.

## B

**Beta Prior**: A probability distribution on [0, 1] used for ownership share parameters.

**Book Value**: The value of an asset according to its balance sheet.

## C

**Capitalised Earnings**: Valuation method applying P/E multiples to firm profit.

**CI/CD**: Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery.

**Content-Addressed DAG**: A directed acyclic graph where tasks are cached based on input hashes.

## D

**DAG**: Directed Acyclic Graph.

**Decomposition**: Breaking down inequality into component contributions.

**DNA Adjustment**: Distributional National Accounts adjustment to align micro-data with macro totals.

**DCO**: Developer Certificate of Origin.

## E

**ELBO**: Evidence Lower Bound, used in variational inference.

**EUPL-1.2**: European Union Public Licence version 1.2.

## F

**FAIR**: Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable principles.

**Fåmansföretag**: Closely-held company in Swedish tax law.

## G

**Gini Coefficient**: A standard measure of inequality ranging from 0 to 1.

**GNN**: Graph Neural Network.

**GPD**: General Data Protection Regulation.

## H

**HeteroGAT**: Heterogeneous Graph Attention Network.

**Heterogeneous Graph**: A graph with multiple node and edge types.

**Household**: A unit of analysis for wealth measurement.

## I

**Inequality Index**: A measure of wealth distribution inequality.

**ISIN**: International Securities Identification Number.

**IWAE**: Importance Weighted Autoencoder bound.

## K

**Kinship Edge**: Edge representing familial relationships in the graph.

## L

**Listed Equity**: Equity traded on a public stock exchange.

**Lorenz Curve**: Graphical representation of wealth distribution.

## M

**Mean-Field Posterior**: Variational posterior assuming independence between parameters.

**Macro Total**: Aggregate wealth from national accounts.

## N

**NACE**: Statistical Classification of Economic Activities in the European Community.

**Negative Edge Sampling**: Generating non-existent edges for link prediction training.

## O

**Ownership Chain**: Chain of ownership relationships between entities.

**Ownership Share**: Fraction of an entity owned by another entity.

## P

**Pareto Tail**: Power-law distribution modeling the upper tail of wealth distribution.

**P/E Multiple**: Price-to-earnings ratio used in valuation.

**Polars**: Data manipulation library used in the package.

## Q

**Quantile**: A value below which a given percentage of observations fall.

## R

**RGCN**: Relational Graph Convolutional Network.

**Register Data**: Official administrative data from government sources.

## S

**SCB**: Statistics Sweden (Statistiska centralbyrån).

**Seed**: A value used to initialize random number generators.

**Skatteverket**: Swedish Tax Agency.

**Stochastic**: Random or probabilistic.

## T

**Theil Index**: Entropy-based inequality measure.

**Top Share**: Fraction of wealth held by top quantile of population.

**Taxeringsvärde**: Tax assessment value of real estate in Sweden.

## U

**Unlisted Equity**: Equity not traded on a public stock exchange.

## V

**Variational Inference**: Approximate Bayesian inference using optimization.

**Valuation**: Estimating the market value of an asset.

## W

**Wealth Register**: Administrative data on household wealth.

## Z

**Zenodo**: Open science platform for research artifacts.
