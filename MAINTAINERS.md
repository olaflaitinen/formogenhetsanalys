## Responsibilities

The lead maintainer is responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests.
- Triaging and closing issues.
- Publishing releases and signing artefacts.
- Maintaining EUPL-1.2 licence compliance.
- Responding to security vulnerability reports within 90 days.
- Submitting the repository to the Interoperable Europe Portal.
- Maintaining the Zenodo deposition record.
- Liaison with Stockholm University research-data infrastructure.
- Liaison with SCB (Statistiska Centralbyran) for secure-environment access.
